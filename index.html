About

My name is Amy Clemens and I like drawing things. I’m a part-time illustrator and full-time graphic designer based in Melbourne, Australia.
I graduated in 2012 from Swinburne University of Technology with a Bachelor Degree in Communication Design (Graphic Design) (Honours) and I’m currently working full-time as a graphic designer who specialises in packaging design and branding.
In my spare time I like to draw people who inspire me, people who intrigue me and people who have great stories to tell.
