About

My name is Amy Clemens and I like drawing things. I am a part-time illustrator and full-time graphic designer based in Melbourne, Australia.

I graduated in 2012 from Swinburne University of Technology with a Bachelor Degree in Communication Design (Graphic Design) (Honours) and I am currently working full-time as a graphic designer who specialises in packaging design and branding.

In my spare time I like to draw people who inspire me, people who intrigue me and people who have great stories to tell.

FAQ

What materials do you use?

I use Prismacolour coloured pencils and Faber Castell greylead pencils. The paper I use is Bristol drawing board. I also use a range of other tools such as fineliners, gel pens, paint and charcoal. For my typography work I use a range of brush pens.

The print I love has sold out, will you print more?

Unfortunately, no. Once a print has sold out it’s gone forever. Each print is individually numbered which makes them more special and unique.

Do you take commission work?

I do not take any personal commission work. However, if you have a professional project you would be interested in chatting about, please get in contact. I’m currently working full-time as a graphic designer so I can only take on very limited amounts of commission work.

Is your work copyrighted?

Yes, all of my work is copyrighted. Any reproduction or use of my artwork without permission us against the law.

Do you do custom prints?

Yes! Please get in contact with me to discuss any custom prints.
